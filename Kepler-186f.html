Kepler-186f

Kepler-186f is a significant exoplanet discovery due to its similarity to Earth.


Key characteristics
  • Type:The exact composition of Kepler-186f is unknown, but it's believed to be a rocky planet.

  • Mass: It's the first Earth-sized planet discovered in the habitable zone of another star. This means it could potentially support liquid water on its surface.

  • Location: It orbits Kepler-186, a red dwarf star located about 490 light-years from Earth.

  • Orbital period: 130 days.

  • Temperature: While it's within the habitable zone, its temperature is likely lower than Earth's due to its star being a red dwarf.
